About FreezCool Technical LLC

FreezCool Technical LLC is a trusted refrigeration and cold storage solutions provider based in Ajman, UAE. We specialize in the design, manufacture, installation, and maintenance of industrial and commercial refrigeration systems.

Trusted By Leading Brands

Who We Are

At FreezCool Technical LLC, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality refrigeration solutions tailored to the requirements of supermarkets, restaurants, food processing facilities, warehouses, pharmaceutical companies, and industrial clients.

Our expertise covers cold rooms, blast freezers, walk-in chillers, centralized refrigeration systems, refrigerated containers, supermarket refrigeration equipment, and air-conditioning systems.

We focus on quality workmanship, efficient project execution, energy-efficient systems, and long-term customer satisfaction.
